**Jan 2023 – Jun 2024 | M.Sc. Thesis, Sharif University of Technology**

Developed an ensemble of Graph Neural Network (GNN) architectures paired with a generative explainer to identify molecular substructures that drive chemical properties.

**Key Contributions:**
- Designed a GNN ensemble for robust chemical property prediction.
- Built a generative explainer to highlight rationales (substructures) behind predictions.
- Proposed statistical evaluation methods and interpretability metrics.
- Validated results through expert chemist review.

**Technologies:** Python, PyTorch, PyTorch Geometric, RDKit, scikit-learn
