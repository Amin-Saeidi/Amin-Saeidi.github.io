**Fall 2021 | Sharif University of Technology**

Performed comprehensive bioinformatics data analysis on microarray gene expression data for acute myeloid leukemia (AML).

**Analysis Steps:**
- Data quality control and normalization of microarray data.
- Dimensionality reduction (PCA, t-SNE) and sample correlation analysis.
- Differential gene expression analysis.
- Gene ontology (GO) and pathway analysis to identify dysregulated biological processes.

**Technologies:** R, Bioconductor, limma, DESeq2, ggplot2, clusterProfiler
