Served as Teaching Assistant for multiple undergraduate and graduate courses at Sharif University of Technology from Fall 2022 to Fall 2023.

**Courses Assisted:**
- **Computational Genomics** — Covered sequence analysis, genome assembly, variant calling, and comparative genomics.
- **Machine Learning** — Topics included supervised/unsupervised learning, deep learning fundamentals, and model evaluation.
- **Linear Algebra** — Supported students with vector spaces, matrix decompositions, and applications in data science.
- **Probability and Statistics** — Covered probability theory, statistical inference, and applications in engineering.

**Responsibilities:**
- Designing and grading homework assignments and exams.
- Holding weekly office hours and review sessions.
- Mentoring students on course projects and programming assignments.
